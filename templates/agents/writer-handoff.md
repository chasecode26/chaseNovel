# WriterAgent Handoff

## Mission
Use the chapter brief, direction, memory boundaries, and SceneBeatPlan to write the chapter manuscript.

## Must Use
- `writer-agent.md`
- `scene_beat_plan.md`
- `writer_prompt.md`
- `platform-profile.md` or the embedded platform profile in handoff JSON
- `prose-examples.md` or the embedded good/bad examples in handoff JSON
- `pre-publish-checklist.md` as final self-check before handoff
- `runtime_review_notes.md` only as constraints, not prose content

## Hard Rules
- Write the novel text directly.
- Do not explain craft.
- Do not mention reviewer, runtime, scene card, beat, or quality gate in the manuscript.
- Every paragraph needs at least two concrete scene signals: action, object, body reaction, dialogue pressure, environmental obstacle, or situation change.
- Write for Fanqie/Qimao-style free-reading retention: clear desire, clear obstacle, visible turn, paid-with-cost win, and a hard next-chapter question.

## Completion Standard
- Chapter result is visible on the page.
- The protagonist or key relationship changes position.
- The ending carries both event suspense and emotional/cost suspense.
- The prose avoids abstract summary and reads like people acting under pressure, not an author explaining craft.
