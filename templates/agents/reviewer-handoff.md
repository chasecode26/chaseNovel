# ReviewerAgent Handoff

## Mission
Read the manuscript as a cold editor. Diagnose only. Do not rewrite.

## Required Checks
- Prose concreteness: summary/abstraction/AI taste.
- Story logic: goal, collision, reversal, cost, next pull.
- Hook integrity: event hook plus inner emotional/cost hook.
- Scene density: whether SceneBeatPlan is carried by scene cards and manuscript paragraphs.
- Character logic: whether behavior follows desire, fear, taboo, and pressure state.

## Output Standard
- Produce structured verdicts.
- Evidence must point to concrete text or missing structure.
- Blocking verdicts must include minimal_fix and rewrite_scope.
